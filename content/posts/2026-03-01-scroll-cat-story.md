## Adding Character To Reading

A tiny character can make scrolling feel less mechanical.

## Scroll-Linked Animation

Map document progress to rope length, then trigger a reward interaction at the bottom.

### Bottom-Reached Reward

When users finish an article, trigger one short celebratory jump and message bubble.

## Design Constraint

Do not block reading flow. Delight should be optional and lightweight.
